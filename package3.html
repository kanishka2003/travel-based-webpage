Top Destination

SUMMER

Goa

Goa is all about sun, sea and sand.Goa carries a vast spectrum of cultural practices.Goa's weather will warm your heart if you are a nature's lover.

Munnar

Munnar is a blissful tourist destination in Kerala, sanctified with serenity, an enjoyable climate, numerous sightseeing and impressive scenery. It has been a very famous tourist resort right from the British era.

Ladakhi

Ladakh is a region located in the northernmost part of India.It is a popular destination for trekking and mountaineering, with several challenging routes that offer stunning views of the Himalayas

Shimla

Shimla is a well-known hill station in Northern India and is one of the most famous vacation places in summer that perfectly captures Indian magnificence with British elegance. It is bequeathed with a rich history and is sanctified with natural beauty. If you’re looking for a relaxing yet fun retreat, Shimla has to be on your list of coolest places to visit in India in May and June

Manali

A holiday in Manali conjures up images of green mountains amidst the snow-covered ranges, a breath of fresh air and a relaxed vacation. Manali is among the most beautiful places to visit in India in summer. It is bequeathed with mercerizing scenery, lush greenery, and miraculous flora and fauna.

Wayanad

Wayanad is a scenic and tranquil district located in Kearala. Wayanad is famous for its sprawling tea and coffee plantations, which produce some of the finest tea and coffee in the country. Wayanad has a rich history and cultural heritage, with several ancient temples

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a popular hill station located in the Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu, India. Kodaikanal also offers opportunities for trekking, boating, cycling, horse riding, and exploring the nearby hills and forests.

Sikkim

Sikkim is a northeastern state in India, bordered by Nepal to the west, Bhutan to the east, and Tibet (China) to the north and northeast.Sikkim offers a serene and peaceful environment, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those interested in experiencing unique cultures.

Cherrapunji

Cherrapunji, also known as Sohra, is a town located in the East Khasi Hills district of Meghalaya, India. It is renowned for being one of the wettest places on Earth, receiving exceptionally high rainfall throughout the year. Cherrapunji's unique climate and natural beauty make it a popular tourist destination.

WINTER

Auli

Auli gets a winter fairytale look with snow in every direction. The main attraction of Auli during winter is skiing. Apart from skiing, Auli is also famous for other tourist activities, like snow trekking, view from Kwani Bugyal, riding the longest cable car of the continent, catching a panoramic view of snow peaks of Himalayas, and so on.

Gir national park

Gir forest of Gujarat is the only place in India where you can see lions in their natural habitat. Winters are considered to be the best time to be at jungles.While Kutch is a unique land lying over the Tropic of Cancer offering wonderful handicrafts and is also a blend of varied customs, folk songs and dances, the 2000 years old Buddhist caves of Uparkot exhibits the serene lifestyle of the monks.

Corbyns cove beach

Corbyn's Cove Beach is known for its stunning scenic beauty, with crystal clear blue waters, soft white sand, and lush green vegetation all around. It's a great spot to relax and enjoy the natural surroundings. Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood, local delicacies, and refreshing drinks while taking in the beautiful views of the beach.

Meenakshi amman temple

The temple state of Tamil Nadu is a gem of temple architecture, with the shrines like Meenakshi Temple, Brihadeeswarar temple, Kapaleeshwarar temple and the Chola temples; which are just not worship spaces but hot seat of Dravidian designs.

Tawang

Tawang is an offbeat hill station situated in the North-eastern state of Arunachal Pradesh. It is undoubtedly the most difficult places to reach in winters, from this list, but once you capture the glorious views of the snow-covered Himalayas, valleys wrapped by colourful prayer flags and ancient monasteries, the efforts put in would seem worthwhile.

Kashmir

Gulmarg in Kashmir is a favorite destination for snow lovers. In this trip, we would recommend you to spend 2 nights 3 days only in Gulmarg because there is so much to do here. Sports like skiing, sledding, snow scooters will not fill your life. At the same time, it is fun to make your snowman with yourselves so high above the earth.

Thar desert

The Thar Desert also known as the Great Indian Desert, is an arid region in the north-western part of the Indian subcontinent that covers an area of 205,000 km (79,000 sq mi) in India and Pakistan. It is the world's 20th-largest desert, and the world's 9th-largest hot subtropical desert.

Ooty

Ooty is a hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. It is also known as Udagamandalam. Ooty is a popular tourist destination and is known for its scenic beauty and pleasant weather.

Coorg

Madikeri or Mercara, the capital of Coorg is a beautiful large hill town situated in Western Ghats. This hill station stands 1452 meters above sea level. The green woody slopes, colourful scenery, misty mountains, cascading falls, dense tropical forest, tea, coffee and spice plantations made this place a paradise of nature lovers.

SPRING

Darjeeling

Darjeeling is located in West Bengal is just another stop to cool off the summer.The valley covered with rhododendron flowers and clear blue sky with jaw-dropping views to snow-covered Kanchenjunga is everything you would want from Darjeeling.

Valley of flowers

The beautiful meadow located in the foothills of Nanda Devi is the most amazing thing to see in spring. Here you can find an exotic range of flora like Brahma Kamal, the blue poppy, and the cobra lily.

Ziro valley

Ziro Valley is a town in Arunachal Pradesh. Its Apatani cultural landscape is included in the Tentative List of UNESCO World Heritage Site. Travellers can plan a visit to Meghna Cave Temple, Ziro Plut,Tarin Fish Farm, Talley Valley and Kile Pakho.

Amritsar

Beautiful mustard fields and striking holy places make the landscape of Amritsar breathtaking. The city is the economic and cultural centre in the Majha region of the state. It is also one of the holiest cities in India for the followers of the Sikh religion. Tourists can plan a visit to the Golden Temple, Partition Museum, Mandir Mata Lal Devi, Wagah Border, Baba Atal Tower, Khalsa College, Shri Durgiana Mandir, Jallianwala Bagh and Ram Bagh Gardens.

Dayara bugyal

Dayara Bugyal offers a stunning view. It is located at an elevation of 3639m and has rich fauna and flora. It offers a picturesque view of snow-capped Garhwal Himalayas, including Bandarpunch, Nanda Devi and Swargarohini. You can visit the nearby hamlets or go trekking. In the spring, the place boasts beautiful meadows filled with greenery and flowers. It is also one of the renowned camping destinations in Uttrakhand.

Sandakphu

The highest peak in the state of West Bengal, Sandakphu is located adjacent to the Singalila National Park and gives visitors the best possible view of Kanchenjunga. What sets Sandakphu apart is the fact that it gives you clear views of four of the five highest peaks in the world- Everest, Kanchenjunga, Makalu, and Lhotse.For people interested in trekking, the trek, combined with the brilliant views and lush greenery, makes Sandakphu one of the most beautiful places to visit during Spring in India.

Araku valley

Araku Valley is located in Vishakhapatnam district, Andhra Pradesh and being surrounded by the magnificent Eastern Ghats the place hosts refreshing breaks for nature lovers. The valley is known for its exquisite biodiversity and features lush green tea and coffee plantations.

Bir

Bir is located in the Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh. This picturesque scenery area is known for eco-tourism and meditation center. During spring Bir becomes the best self and offers a lot of activities to explore like hiking, paragliding, etc

Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar is a plateau surrounded by valleys and greenery. This was used to be the summer capital of Bombay province. This place is the birthplace to the river Khrisna river and gets the gift of nature complimentary.

MONSOON

Lonavala

Lonavala is a popular hill station. It is situated in the Sahyadri mountain range and is known for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, and lush green surroundings.

Coorg

Coorg is known for its stunning natural beauty, including rolling hills, verdant forests, sparkling waterfalls, and meandering rivers. Coorg is also famous for its honey.Nagarhole National Park, which is a popular spot for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Meghalayas

Meghalaya is famous for its unique living root bridges, which are made by training the roots of rubber trees to grow across rivers and streams.The Krem Liat Prah cave is one of the longest in the world and is a popular destination for caving enthusiasts.

Promenade

Promenades around the world are famous for their scenic beauty, cultural significance, and the opportunity they offer for walking, strolling, and enjoying the atmosphere of the place.

Abu

Mount Abu is the highest peak in the Aravalli Range and is a popular destination for trekking, hiking, and sightseeing.Its cultural landmarks such as the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.

Jog falls

Jog Falls is also known as Gerosoppa Falls, is a famous waterfall. It is one of the highest waterfalls in India, with a total height of 829 feet

Amboli

Amboli is a hill station in south Maharashtra, India. At an altitude of 690 m (2,260 ft) it is the last hill station before the coastal highlands of Goa. Amboli lies in the Sahayadri Hills of Western India, one of the world's "Eco Hot-Spots" and it abounds in unusual flora and fauna.

Agumbe

Agumbe is a small village located in the Shimoga district of the Indian state of Karnataka.Agumbe's ecological importance, scenic beauty, wildlife, and cultural connections make it a unique destination for nature enthusiasts and those seeking an off-the-beaten-path experience in the lap of pristine rainforests.

Chikmagalur

Chikmagalur is a picturesque hill town located in the state of Karnataka, India.Chikmagalur's natural beauty, coffee plantations, wildlife, and religious sites make it a popular destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those seeking a peaceful retreat in the lap of nature.

AUTUMN

Utrecht

Shades of yellow, red and orange embellish the ancient canals of Utrecht.The canals with their wharves and wharf cellars are one of a kind in the world.The wharf cellars have been transformed into cosy cafes, bars, restaurants and shops. The best way to take in this Dutch wonder is by boat. Rent a boat and paddle your way through the colourful canals. If it gets a bit chilly, stop by for a warm beverage along the way.

Novosibirsk

Novosibirsk deserves to be discovered especially in light of the last rays of autumn sun. The golden sunset sublimes the silver dome and massive columns of the Opera and Ballet Theatre, the largest in Russia. It’s hard not to feel humbled besides such massive architecture, guarded by Lenin’s imposing statue on Lenina Square. Brave locals might still bathe in the waters of the Ob River, so join in or grab a blanket and sit on the shore watching the Trans-Siberian train chug across the Novosibirsk Rail Bridge.

Belfast

The nip of winter shyly shows the tip of its nose behind the maple autumn coloured scenery. Towering over Belfast, hike up Black Mountain for a view that stretches across the city towards the Strangford Lough inlet. On your way to visit Old Bushmills Distillery, a whiskey-making legend in Northern Ireland, walk beneath the Dark Hedges, intertwined beech trees hugging the road.

Ontario

Ontario's largest city is a major cultural and financial center, known for its diverse neighborhoods.Ontario is known as the birthplace of ice hockey, and the sport remains a popular pastime in the province.

Mount fuji

Mount Fuji is a popular destination for hikers and climbers.Mount Fuji is one of the most beautiful natural landmarks in Japan. Its symmetrical cone shape, snow-capped peak, and the surrounding landscape make it a favorite destination for photographers and nature lovers.

Patagonia

Patagonia is known for its spectacular natural scenery, which includes snow-capped mountains, glaciers, forests, and lakes. Patagonia is a popular destination for adventure sports, such as hiking, trekking, climbing, kayaking, and skiing.

Iceland

Stunning Natural Landscapes,Northern Lights (Aurora Borealis),Geothermal Energy,Blue Lagoon,Viking Heritage,Clean Environment.These are just a few highlights, but Iceland offers much more to discover, making it a truly remarkable destination.

Markt hartmannsdorf

Markt Hartmannsdorf is a municipality located in Styria, Austria. While it is a relatively small town, it does not have widespread international fame or recognition. famous attractions, landmarks, or features specifically associated with Markt Hartmannsdorf.

Leh

Leh is a town located in the Leh district of the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.Magnetic Hill,Adventure Tourism,Stunning Natural Beauty.
