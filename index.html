Top Destination

Goa

Goa is all about sun, sea and sand. Goa is popular for its range of seafood delicacy. Goa's weather will warm your heart if you are a nature's lover.

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its traditional Ayurvedic treatments.Kerala's backwaters are a network of interconnected canals, lagoons, and lakes that run parallel to the Arabian Sea coast.

Mysore

The Mysore Palace is a grand palace built in the Indo-Saracenic style and is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Mysore. It is home to the Chamundeshwari Temple.
